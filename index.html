Cybersecurity Tips

Use strong & unique passwords for each account.
Enable two-factor authentication wherever possible.
Keep your devices and apps updated.
Do not click on suspicious links or emails.
Regularly backup your important data.
Avoid using public Wi-Fi for banking or transactions.
Review privacy settings on social media.
Use HTTPS websites for online payments.
Educate yourself about common cyber threats.
Install and update antivirus & firewall software.
