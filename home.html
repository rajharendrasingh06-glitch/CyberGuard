Cybersecurity Tips

Use strong, unique passwords for each account.
Enable Two-Factor Authentication wherever possible.
Regularly update devices and applications.
Avoid clicking on suspicious links or attachments.
Backup your data regularly.
Secure your Wi-Fi network with strong passwords.
Be cautious when using public Wi-Fi.
Educate yourself about phishing and social engineering attacks.

About Cybersecurity

Cybersecurity is the practice of defending computers, servers, mobile devices, networks, and data from malicious attacks.
It is also known as IT security or electronic information security.
Cyber threats include phishing emails, malware infections, ransomware, and social engineering attacks.
Key Domains: Network Security, Application Security, InfoSec, Operational Security, Disaster Recovery, End-user Education.
Awareness is the first step towards protection. Think Before You Click!
Updated software and antivirus reduce vulnerabilities.
Strong password management is crucial for online safety.
Regular backup and secure cloud storage protect against data loss.
